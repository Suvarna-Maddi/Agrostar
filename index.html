AGRO STAR

Description:
Agrostar is a premium organic fertilizer specially crafted through a blend of anaerobic and aerobic decomposition processes of agricultural residues. This natural formulation enriches the soil, ensuring sustainable and healthy crop growth.
Key Features:
Powerful Microbial Support: Each 100 gm of Agrostar is infused with 3 crore (30 million) beneficial microorganisms. These microorganisms enhance nutrient absorption and boost plant health.
Nutrient-Rich Composition: Crafted from naturally decomposed agricultural residues, Agrostar provides a balanced mix of essential nutrients to the soil.
Eco-Friendly Solution: A sustainable, chemical-free fertilizer designed to nurture both your crops and the environment.


Dosage: 1-2 gm per liter of water
